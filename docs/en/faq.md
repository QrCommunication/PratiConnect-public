# Frequently asked questions

---

## Account and login

### How do I create a PratiConnect account?

Go to [praticonnect.com](https://praticonnect.com) and click **Start for free**. Enter your professional email, then verify your account through the link sent to your inbox.

### I forgot my password

On the login page, click **Forgot password**, enter your email address, and check your inbox (including spam). The link takes you to a page where you can set a new password.

### Can I use PratiConnect on multiple devices?

Yes. Your account works on desktop, tablet, and smartphone. Data syncs automatically between your devices.

### How do I update my professional information?

Open **Settings > Professional profile**. Edit the fields you need, then click **Save**.

### How do I change my login email address?

In **Settings > Professional profile**, edit the **Email** field. A verification email is sent to the new address. Until you confirm it, the old address remains active.

### How do I cancel my subscription?

Send your request to support@praticonnect.com. Your data is kept for 30 days before permanent deletion, in accordance with the GDPR.

### Can I switch to a different plan?

Yes. In **Settings > Subscription**, click **Change plan**. The change takes effect at the next billing period.

### How do I view my PratiConnect invoices?

In **Settings > Subscription > Billing history**. Each invoice can be downloaded as a PDF.

## Patients

### How do I add a patient?

Click **Patients** in the sidebar, then **+ New patient**. Fill in the required information and confirm with **Create patient**.

### How do I import patients from another software?

In **Settings > Import/Export**, import a CSV file. A template file is available on that page to help you format your data correctly (expected columns: last name, first name, email, phone, date of birth).

### How do I delete a patient?

Open the patient record, click the three-dot menu in the top right corner, then **Delete patient**. Confirm in the dialog. Deletion is permanent.

### How do I archive a patient instead of deleting them?

Open the patient record, click the three-dot menu, then **Archive**. The patient disappears from the active list but their data is kept. You can find them using the **Archived** filter.

### How do I merge two duplicate patient records?

Contact support at support@praticonnect.com with the IDs of both records. The operation is irreversible and requires manual validation.

### How do I share a patient record with a colleague?

Open the patient record and click **Share**. Enter your colleague's email (they must have a PratiConnect account), set the permissions (read-only or edit), and confirm.

### How do I comply with the right to erasure (GDPR)?

Open the patient record, click **GDPR actions**, then **Anonymize record**. Identifying data is removed. The anonymized history is kept for your accounting obligations.

### How do I export a patient's data?

Open the patient record, click **GDPR actions**, then **Export data**. A file containing all of the patient's data is generated in JSON or PDF format.

### How do I configure active languages for a patient?

Open the patient record, **Preferences** section. Check the languages in which you want to send questionnaires and notifications. Available languages depend on your own configuration in **Settings > Active languages**.

## Sessions

### What is the difference between a session and an appointment?

An **appointment** is a time slot reserved in the calendar. A **session** is the consultation itself: clinical notes, procedures performed, patient signature.

### How do I start a session?

From the dashboard, find today's appointment and click **Start**. The duration timer starts automatically.

### Are notes auto-saved?

Yes. Auto-save runs every 30 seconds. A brief **Saving...** indicator appears at the top of the editor.

### How do I add vital signs?

During the session, open the **Vitals** tab. Enter the measured values (blood pressure, weight, temperature, etc.). Recording is automatic.

### How do I get the patient's signature at the end of a session?

1. Click **End** at the end of the session.
2. Review the summary with the patient.
3. Present the signature area (touch screen).
4. The patient signs with their finger, then you click **Validate signature**.

### Can I edit a completed session?

You can add to the notes after the fact: open the session in the **Sessions** section and edit the notes. Changes are tracked in the history. However, procedures and the signature remain locked after validation.

### How do I duplicate a previous session?

Open the session you want to reproduce and click **Duplicate**. A new session is created with the same notes and procedures, linked to the same patient. Adjust the details before confirming.

### How do I use note templates?

In the notes editor, click **Insert template**. Select an existing template from your custom templates or the default ones. The content is inserted at the cursor position.

## Calendar

### How do I sync Google Calendar?

Open **Settings > Integrations** and click **Connect Google Calendar**. Authorize access, then select the calendars to sync. Your personal events will appear as unavailable slots in PratiConnect.

### Does Google Calendar sync work both ways?

Appointments created in PratiConnect appear in Google Calendar. Events created in Google Calendar appear as unavailable slots in PratiConnect, but do not create PratiConnect appointments.

### How do I block a time slot?

In the calendar, click the slot you want to block. Select **Block**, add a reason if you like, and confirm. Patients will not be able to book that slot.

### How do I create a recurring appointment?

Create an appointment as usual, then check **Recurring appointment**. Set the frequency (weekly, biweekly, monthly) and the end date or number of occurrences.

### A patient did not show up -- what should I do?

Open the appointment and click **Mark as no-show**. Choose whether to bill for the absence or not. The **No-show** status is recorded in the patient's history.

### How do I set up appointment reminders?

Reminders are sent automatically: 48 hours before by email, 2 hours before by SMS (if the patient's phone number is on file). To change these intervals, open **Settings > Notifications**.

### How do I change my office hours?

In **Settings > Calendar > Hours**, set your availability for each day of the week. Slots outside these hours will not be offered to patients.

### How do I manage public holidays?

In **Settings > Calendar > Public holidays**, enable the public holidays for your country. You can also add custom closures (vacation, training) with start and end dates.

## Billing

### How do I generate an invoice?

From a completed session, click **Invoice**. Review the line items, select the payment method, and click **Generate invoice**.

### What payment methods are accepted?

Three methods are available: online credit card (Stripe), payment terminal (Viva Wallet), and cash/check (manual recording). Configuration is done in **Settings > Billing > Payment methods**.

### How do I collect an online card payment?

Click **Collect card payment**. The patient enters their card details directly on the secure Stripe page. Confirmation is instant.

### How do I send an invoice by email?

Open the invoice, click **Send by email**, verify the patient's address, and confirm. The patient receives a PDF attachment.

### How do I create a session package?

Open **Billing > Packages**, click **+ Create package**. Set the number of sessions, the total price, the validity period, and save.

### How do I handle an unpaid invoice?

In **Billing**, filter by the **Pending** status. Open the invoice. Three options: send a reminder email, mark as paid (if payment was received through another channel), or cancel the invoice.

### How do I export invoices for accounting?

Open **Billing > Export**. Select the period, choose the format (CSV or PDF), and click **Download**.

### How do I customize the invoice template?

In **Settings > Billing > Template**, you can add your logo, edit the header, footer, and legal notices that appear on each invoice.

### How do I issue a credit note?

Open the invoice and click **Create credit note**. The credit note is generated with a distinct number and references the original invoice.

### Is invoice numbering automatic?

Yes. Invoices are numbered sequentially. The number format is configurable in **Settings > Billing > Numbering**.

## Video consultation

### What are the technical requirements?

A modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge), a working camera and microphone, and a stable internet connection (2 Mbps minimum).

### How do I start a video consultation?

Create an appointment of the **Video consultation** type. On the appointment day, click **Start video consultation** from the dashboard. Allow camera and microphone access, then wait for the patient to join.

### How does the patient get the connection link?

The link is automatically included in the appointment confirmation email. You can resend it manually from the appointment details by clicking **Resend link**.

### Can I record a video consultation?

Yes, with the patient's explicit consent. During the consultation, click **Record**. The patient receives a consent request on screen. If they accept, recording starts. The file is stored encrypted.

### Can I share my screen during the consultation?

Yes. Click the **Share screen** button in the toolbar and select the window or screen to broadcast.

### The patient's connection is poor -- what should I do?

Advise them to move closer to their router, turn off video to keep audio only, or switch to a 4G/5G connection. If the problem persists, you can call them by phone instead.

### How do I send a questionnaire during a video consultation?

During the consultation, click **Send questionnaire**. The patient receives a notification in their browser and can answer in real time.

### What happens if the connection drops?

Refresh the page or leave and rejoin the session. The consultation stays open as long as both parties have not left. No data is lost.

## Questionnaires

### How do I create a custom questionnaire?

Open **Questionnaires > + Create**. Drag and drop question types (text, single choice, multiple choice, scale, date) into the form. Configure each question (label, options, required status), test with **Preview**, then click **Save**.

### How do I send a questionnaire to a patient?

From the patient record, click **Send questionnaire** and select the questionnaire. The patient receives an email with a link to respond.

### How do I send a questionnaire to multiple patients?

In the **Questionnaires** section, select the questionnaire, click **Bulk send**, then check the patients you want to include. Each patient receives an individual link.

### How do I view a patient's responses?

Open the patient record, **Questionnaires** tab. Click the response you want to view. Answers are displayed question by question.

### How do I track response trends over time?

In the **Questionnaires** tab of the patient record, a trend chart appears for questionnaires with scoring enabled. Each submission is a point on the curve.

### How do I have a patient fill out a questionnaire during a session?

During the session, open the **Questionnaires** tab and click **Fill out now**. The patient can answer on your screen (tablet) or on their own phone via a link sent by SMS.

### Can I create reusable questionnaire templates?

Yes. Any saved questionnaire becomes a reusable template. You can also duplicate an existing questionnaire to adapt it.

### How do I set up questionnaire scoring?

In the questionnaire editor, enable **Scoring** in the advanced settings. Assign a weight to each possible answer. The total score is calculated automatically with each submission.

## Documents

### What file formats are accepted?

Images: JPG, PNG, GIF. Documents: PDF, DOC, DOCX. Maximum size: 10 MB per file.

### How do I upload a document to a patient's record?

Open the patient record, **Documents** tab, then click **+ Add document**. Select the file from your device. The document is attached to the patient's record.

### How do I share a document with a patient?

Open the patient record, **Documents** tab. Select the document, then enable the **Visible in patient portal** option. The patient will see it in their **Documents** section.

### How do I generate a session report as a PDF?

At the end of the session, click **Generate report**. Choose a template from those you have configured. The PDF is created and attached to the patient's record.

### How do I request an electronic signature?

Upload the document, click **Request signature**, place the signature fields on the document, and send the request to the patient. The patient signs from their portal. The signature is eIDAS-compliant.

### How do I organize documents by category?

When adding a document, select a category (prescription, report, consent, other). You can filter by category in the patient's **Documents** tab.

### How much storage space is available?

Storage space depends on your subscription plan. You can check your usage in **Settings > Storage**.

## Patient portal

### How do I activate the portal for a patient?

Open the patient record, make sure their email is on file, then click **Activate patient portal**. The patient receives an invitation email to create their password.

### What can a patient do on the portal?

Depending on your configuration: view their appointments, book appointments online, fill out questionnaires, view and download their documents, pay their invoices, join a video consultation.

### How do I deactivate the portal for a patient?

Open the patient record, **Patient portal** section, and click **Deactivate**. The patient will no longer be able to log in. Their data is kept.

### The patient did not receive the invitation -- what should I do?

Verify the patient's email in their record. Ask them to check their spam folder. If the problem persists, resend the invitation from the patient record by clicking **Resend invitation**.

### How do I configure what the patient sees on the portal?

In **Settings > Patient portal**, enable or disable the available features: online booking, document access, online payment, questionnaire access.

### Can the patient edit their personal information?

Yes, from the **Profile** section of their portal. They can change their phone number, address, and photo. Medical information can only be edited by the practitioner.

## Security

### How is data protected?

Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest (AES-256). Hosting is HDS-certified (Health Data Hosting) in France. Encrypted backups are performed daily.

### Who can access my patients' data?

Only you, and colleagues you have explicitly shared a record with. The PratiConnect team does not have access to your patient data.

### How does PratiConnect comply with the GDPR?

Explicit patient consent is recorded. Right of access, rectification, and erasure is supported. Data export on request, anonymization option, configurable retention policy.

### How do I enable two-factor authentication?

In **Settings > Security**, enable **Two-factor authentication**. You will receive a code by SMS or through an authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy) at each login.

### I suspect unauthorized access -- what should I do?

Change your password immediately. In **Settings > Security > Active sessions**, review the list of connected devices and disconnect any suspicious sessions. Contact support at support@praticonnect.com.

### How do I permanently delete a patient's data?

Open the patient record, click **GDPR actions > Delete permanently**. This action is irreversible: all patient data, including sessions and documents, is deleted.

### Is data backed up automatically?

Yes. Encrypted backups are performed daily and kept for 30 days. In the event of an incident, data can be restored from any backup point.

## Technical

### Which browsers are supported?

Google Chrome (recommended), Mozilla Firefox, Apple Safari, and Microsoft Edge. Use the latest version of your browser.

### The application is slow -- what should I do?

Clear your browser cache, check your internet connection, close unused tabs, and try a different browser. If the problem persists, contact support with your browser and operating system details.

### I cannot log in

Make sure your email and password are correct. Use **Forgot password** if needed. Clear your browser cache and cookies. Try in a private/incognito window.

### The screen stays blank after login

Clear your browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+Delete on Chrome/Firefox, Cmd+Shift+Delete on Safari). If the problem persists, disable browser extensions and try again.

### How do I report a bug?

Send an email to support@praticonnect.com including a description of the problem, the steps to reproduce it, a screenshot if possible, and your browser and operating system.

### Is there a mobile app?

A native mobile app is under development. In the meantime, PratiConnect works on tablet and smartphone through the web browser.

### What bandwidth is required?

For standard use (patient management, calendar, billing): 1 Mbps is enough. For video consultation: 2 Mbps minimum recommended.

### Is data accessible offline?

No. PratiConnect requires an active internet connection. However, data entered in forms is saved locally during brief disconnections and synced when the connection is restored.

### How do I update PratiConnect?

PratiConnect is a web application: updates are deployed automatically. No action is required on your part. Release notes are available in **Settings > What's new**.
