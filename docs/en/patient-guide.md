# Access your patient portal

The patient portal is your personal space on PratiConnect. You can view your appointments, questionnaires, documents, and invoices from any browser on a computer, tablet, or smartphone.

**Prerequisite**: your practitioner must have activated the patient portal for your account. You will then receive an invitation email with a login link, valid for 24 hours.

---

## 1. Log in

1. Open the email sent by PratiConnect (check your spam folder if needed).
2. Click the **Access my portal** button.
3. On the page that opens, create your password:
   - 8 characters minimum
   - at least one uppercase letter
   - at least one number
4. Enter it again in the **Confirm password** field.
5. Click **Create my account**.

You land directly on your dashboard.

**For future logins**: go to the PratiConnect login page, enter your email and password, then click **Log in**.

**Forgot your password**: on the login page, click **Forgot password**, enter your email, and follow the link you receive to set a new one.

---

## 2. Explore the patient portal

The dashboard shows four main blocks:

| Block | Content displayed |
|-------|-------------------|
| Next appointment | Date, time, and type of your upcoming consultation |
| To do | Questionnaires waiting for your response |
| Documents | Latest documents shared by your practitioner |
| Invoices | Outstanding amounts to pay |

**Navigation**:

- On mobile, sections appear at the bottom of the screen.
- On desktop, a sidebar lists the entries: **Home**, **Appointments**, **Documents**, **Profile**.

To update your personal information (phone, address), open **Profile**, edit the relevant fields, and click **Save**. Some medical information can only be edited by your practitioner.

---

## 3. Book an appointment

This feature is available if your practitioner has enabled online booking.

1. Open the **Appointments** section and click **Book an appointment**.
2. Select the consultation type (follow-up, full assessment, video consultation).
3. Choose a date on the calendar. Available days are in color; fully booked days are grayed out.
4. Select a time slot from those offered.
5. Click **Confirm**.

You receive a confirmation email with the date, time, practice address (or video link for a teleconsultation), and any preparation instructions.

**Cancel an appointment**: in the **Appointments** section, open the appointment and click **Cancel**, then confirm. Some practitioners require a minimum cancellation notice (for example, 48 hours). Beyond that, contact your practitioner directly.

---

## 4. Fill out a questionnaire

Your practitioner can send you questionnaires to prepare for a consultation or track how you are doing over time.

1. On the dashboard, look for the **Questionnaire to fill out** notification.
2. Click **Fill out now**.
3. Answer the questions one by one. Possible types:
   - Free text
   - Single choice (radio button)
   - Multiple choice (checkboxes)
   - Scale (slider)
   - Date (calendar picker)
4. Navigate with the **Previous** and **Next** buttons.
5. Click **Submit** on the last question.

Your answers are auto-saved. If you stop partway through, you will pick up where you left off.

Once submitted, the questionnaire disappears from the **To do** list. Your practitioner reviews your answers before or during the session.

---

## 5. View and pay an invoice

Your practitioner can send you an invoice to pay online, particularly after a video consultation.

1. Click the **Invoice to pay** notification on the dashboard, or open the **Documents** section.
2. Review the amount and the breakdown of services.
3. Click **Pay now**.
4. Enter your credit card details: number, expiration date, security code (CVV).
5. Click **Pay**.

Payment is processed by Stripe. Your card details are never stored on PratiConnect.

You receive a confirmation email. The invoice status changes to **Paid** in your portal.

## Join a video consultation

Before the consultation:
- Make sure your device's camera and microphone are working.
- Choose a quiet, well-lit spot.
- Make sure you have a stable internet connection (2 Mbps minimum).

On the day of the consultation:

1. Log in to your portal a few minutes before the scheduled time.
2. On the dashboard, click **Join video consultation**.
3. Allow camera and microphone access in your browser.
4. Wait in the virtual waiting room.

During the consultation, the buttons at the bottom of the screen control the **microphone** (mute/unmute), the **camera** (on/off), and let you **leave** the call.

If you experience a technical issue, refresh the page or leave and rejoin.

## Data security

- Health data is hosted in France on HDS-certified servers.
- The connection is encrypted (HTTPS). Data at rest is encrypted with AES-256.
- Only you and your practitioner can access your information.

Under the GDPR, you can view, correct, export, or request deletion of your data. To delete your account, contact your practitioner or email support@praticonnect.com.
