# Conduct a full session

This tutorial covers the complete workflow of a care session: from starting it to obtaining the patient's signature.

**Duration**: 10 minutes

## Prerequisites

- At least one patient created in your account
- Your billable services configured in **Settings > Services**

---

### 1. Start the session

**From a scheduled appointment**

- Open your **Dashboard**.
- Find the day's appointment in the list.
- Click **Start**.

**From the patient's profile (walk-in session)**

- Go to **Patients**, then open the relevant profile.
- Click **+ New session**.
- Confirm the date and time, then click **Create and start**.

**Result**: the session interface opens and the timer starts automatically.

---

### 2. Navigate the session interface

The screen is divided into three areas:

- **Header**: patient name (link to their profile), session type, timer, Pause and End buttons.
- **Tab bar**: Summary, Notes, Vitals, Questionnaires, Scales, Medical history, Goals, Prescriptions, Body mapping, Documents, Media, Recording.
- **Main area**: displays the content of the selected tab.

**Result**: you see the Summary tab by default with the running timer.

---

### 3. Write clinical notes

- Select the **Notes** tab.
- Use the text editor to write your observations.
- Structure your notes in sections: reason for visit, clinical examination, treatment performed, recommendations.
- The toolbar offers formatting options: headings, bold, italic, bullet lists, and numbered lists.
- To save time, click the **Templates** icon in the toolbar and choose a predefined note template.

Notes are saved automatically every 30 seconds. A brief "Saving..." indicator appears.

**Result**: your notes are saved and attached to this session.

---

### 4. Record vital signs

- Select the **Vitals** tab.
- Enter the measurements taken during the consultation:

| Measurement | Unit | Format |
|-------------|------|--------|
| Heart rate | bpm | Whole number |
| Blood pressure | mmHg | Two fields: systolic / diastolic |
| Temperature | °C | One decimal (e.g., 36.8) |
| O2 saturation | % | Pulse oximeter value |
| Weight | kg | Number |
| Height | cm | Number |

- BMI is calculated automatically from weight and height.
- Click **Save measurements**.
- Only fill in the measurements you actually took.

**Result**: vital signs appear in the patient's history for longitudinal tracking.

---

### 5. Use body mapping

- Select the **Body mapping** tab.
- Choose the view: front, back, or side.
- Select a tool: **Point** to mark a specific area, **Zone** to draw a surface.
- Choose the color by intensity: green (mild), yellow (moderate), orange (significant), red (severe).
- Click on the body diagram.
- Add a description if needed.

**Result**: visual annotations are saved with the session and viewable in the patient's history.

---

### 6. Add performed services

- Go back to the **Summary** tab.
- In the "Services" section, click **+ Add a service**.
- Select the service from your catalog (e.g., Osteopathy consultation, Strapping).
- Adjust the quantity if needed.
- The total amount is calculated automatically by adding all services.

| Service | Rate | Quantity | Total |
|---------|-----:|:--------:|------:|
| Osteopathy consultation | 60 EUR | 1 | 60 EUR |
| Strapping | 15 EUR | 1 | 15 EUR |
| **Total** | | | **75 EUR** |

Configure your services ahead of time in **Settings > Services** so they are easy to find.

**Result**: the session total is visible in the summary.

---

### 7. Pause or resume

If you need to interrupt the session:

- Click **Pause** in the header. The timer stops.
- To resume, click **Resume**.
- The total duration accounts for pauses.

**Result**: the timer picks up where it left off.

---

### 8. End the session

- Click the **End session** button at the top right.
- A summary window opens.
- Review the session duration, performed services, and total amount.

**Result**: the summary displays with the signature area.

---

### 9. Obtain the patient's signature

- Show the summary to the patient.
- The patient signs in the designated area using their finger (tablet) or mouse.
- If the signature is not satisfactory, click **Clear** to start over.
- Click **Validate signature**.

The signature is timestamped and stored securely. It holds legal value.

**Result**: the signature appears in the session summary.

---

### 10. Validate and finalize

- Click **Validate and finish**.
- The session status changes to "Completed".
- You can now generate an invoice, print a report, or send a document to the patient.

If the patient left without signing, click **Finish without signature** and add a note explaining the reason. A session without a signature carries less legal weight.

**Result**: the completed session appears in the patient's history with all recorded data.
