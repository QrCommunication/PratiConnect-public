# Create your first patient

This tutorial walks you through adding a patient in PratiConnect and completing their profile.

**Duration**: 5 minutes

## Prerequisites

- An active PratiConnect account
- Signed in to your practitioner dashboard

---

### 1. Open the creation form

- In the left sidebar, select **Patients**.
- Click the **+ New patient** button at the top right of the list.
- A 4-step form opens in a modal window.

**Result**: the creation form opens on the "Identity" step.

---

### 2. Fill in the patient's identity

Complete the following fields:

| Field | Required | Example |
|-------|:--------:|---------|
| Title | Yes | Mrs. |
| First name | Yes | Marie |
| Last name | Yes | Dupont |
| Date of birth | Yes | 03/15/1985 |
| Gender | No | Female |

- The date of birth is used to automatically calculate the patient's age on their profile.
- Click **Next** to proceed to the next step.

**Result**: the "Contact details" step appears.

---

### 3. Add contact details

- **Mobile phone**: format +33 6 12 34 56 78. This number is used for appointment SMS reminders.
- **Email**: required to activate the patient portal, send questionnaires, and send invoices.
- **Postal address**: street number, street name, postal code, city, country.
- Click **Next**.

The mobile phone number is the primary channel for automated reminders. Fill it in first.

**Result**: the "Medical information" step appears.

---

### 4. Complete medical information

This step is optional. You can fill it in later from the patient's profile.

**Permanent data**

- Blood type, height (cm), weight (kg).

**Known allergies**

- Click **+ Add an allergy**.
- Enter the allergen name (e.g., Penicillin).
- Choose the severity level: Mild, Moderate, or Severe.
- Describe the observed reaction.

**Current treatments**

- Click **+ Add a treatment**.
- Enter the medication name and dosage (e.g., 1 tablet in the morning).
- Click **Next**.

**Result**: the "Options" step appears.

---

### 5. Configure options

**Custom tags**

- Type a tag name in the search field (e.g., "Monthly follow-up", "Company X").
- Select an existing tag or create a new one.
- Tags help you filter and find your patients quickly.

**Referral source**

- Indicate how the patient found you: word of mouth, website, social media, online directory, or other.
- This information helps you measure the effectiveness of your acquisition channels.

**GDPR consent**

- Check the consent box for health data processing. This checkbox is mandatory.
- PratiConnect automatically records the date, time, and version of the accepted terms of service.

**Result**: a summary displays all the information you entered.

---

### 6. Confirm creation

- Review the summary to verify the information.
- Click **Create patient**.
- The patient's profile opens directly.

**Result**: the patient appears in your list. From their profile, you can schedule an appointment, start a session, or send a questionnaire.
